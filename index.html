About Me

I am passionate about UAV systems, flight dynamics, and autonomous drones. While I have a strong foundation building detailed drone simulations using MATLAB and Simulink, my primary focus is actively engineering the physical systems and control architectures of real-world UAVs. I am deeply involved in hands-on hardware-software integration, bridging the gap between theoretical models and actively flying autonomous systems.

Drone Simulation

  • ✈️

    6 DOF Dynamic Model

    Full nonlinear rigid-body dynamics simulation including translational and rotational motion. Incorporates inertia tensor (Ix, Iy, Iz) and real-time state propagation.

  • 🌪️

    Flight Dynamics & Disturbances

    Simulates aerodynamic effects with external disturbances like wind forces and environmental noise. Includes realistic acceleration modeling using thrust-vector decomposition.

  • 🎯

    Autonomous Control System

    Cascaded PID controller for position and attitude stabilization. Implements waypoint navigation with dynamic trajectory correction.

  • ⚙️

    Motor & Power System

    Models ESC delay (τ = 0.03s) and motor thrust generation (T = kf·ω²). Includes battery voltage drop affecting maximum rotor speed.
